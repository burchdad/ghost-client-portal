- Correct client contact confirmed

- Secure proposal token rotated

- Stripe mode verified

- Project phases verified
- Onboarding form verified
- Client actions verified
- Invitation reviewed
- Invitation email reviewed
- Audit trail checked
- Backup confirmed
- Support contact confirmed
